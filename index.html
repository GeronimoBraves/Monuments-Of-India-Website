Taj Mahal

Commissioned in 1632 by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the remains of his late wife, Mumtaz Mahal, the Taj Mahal is a stunning symbol of love and architectural brilliance.

National Monument

India Gate located in New Delhi is the National Monument of India.

Maximum Monuments

Uttar Pradesh has the maximum monuments in India that count a total number of 3,685 monuments. The place is known as the ‘heartland of India’ due to its cultural heritage and historical relevance.
